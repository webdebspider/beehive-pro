Privacy Policy

Beehive Pro+  ·  WebDebSpider's Designs  ·  Effective: May 4, 2026
Plain English summary: Beehive Pro+ stores your beekeeping data in Firebase (Google's cloud) so it syncs across your devices. We don't sell your data, show you ads, or share your information with third parties. Your data is yours.

1. Information We Collect

Beehive Pro+ collects only the information you provide directly:

  • Account information — your email address and password (stored securely via Firebase Authentication)
  • Hive data — hive names, locations, inspection records, photos, and notes you enter
  • Forage data — GPS coordinates and forage observations you log
  • Voice transcripts — text generated from voice inspections (processed on-device, stored as notes)
  • Photos — images you capture or upload for inspections and AI comb review

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to provide the app's features:

  • Storing and syncing your hive inspection data across devices
  • Enabling AI-powered comb photo analysis (photos are sent to Anthropic's API for analysis only)
  • Displaying your forage locations on the map
  • Offline functionality — data is cached locally on your device

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose other than providing the app.

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored in Google Firebase (Firestore and Firebase Storage), a secure cloud platform with industry-standard encryption in transit and at rest. Photos are stored in Firebase Storage. Authentication is handled by Firebase Authentication.

We take reasonable measures to protect your information, but no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

4. Third-Party Services

Beehive Pro+ uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase — data storage, authentication, and photo hosting (Privacy Policy)
  • Anthropic Claude API — AI comb photo analysis. Photos submitted for analysis are processed according to Anthropic's privacy policy and are not used to train AI models (Privacy Policy)
  • Google Maps — forage location mapping (Privacy Policy)
  • Wikimedia Commons — reference images in the Comb Guide (public domain / CC BY-SA)

5. Location Data

Beehive Pro+ requests access to your device's location only when you use the Forage Log feature. Location data is used solely to record forage entry coordinates. We do not track your location in the background.

6. Camera & Microphone

The app requests access to your camera for taking inspection photos and to your microphone for voice logging. These permissions are used only when you actively use those features.

7. Data Retention & Deletion

Your data is retained as long as you have an account. You may delete your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us at the email below. We will delete your data within 30 days of your request.

8. Children's Privacy

Beehive Pro+ is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the effective date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
