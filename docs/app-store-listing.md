## FULL DESCRIPTION (4000 chars max — works for both stores)

🐝 Beehive Pro+ is the field-ready beekeeping companion built for both beginners finding their footing and experienced keepers who want powerful tools that keep up with them.

**INSPECT SMARTER**
Log inspections with big tap-friendly quick buttons, or just speak — our voice logging detects queen status, brood pattern, mite counts, beetles, and bee temperament from natural speech. No typing, no gloves off.

**AI COMB REVIEW**
Photograph your comb and let AI identify eggs, larvae, capped brood, honey stores, pollen, and warning signs. Get a structured report you can add directly to your inspection log.

**COMB GUIDE**
An interactive field guide for everything on the comb — eggs, larvae, brood patterns, queen cells, honey, and pollen. Beginner mode pulses and auto-expands cards with reference photos as you scroll.

**MENTOR SHARING**
Flag any inspection for expert review and share a formatted summary directly to WhatsApp, Discord, Telegram, email, or SMS with one tap — annotate photos first to highlight exactly what you're seeing.

**PHOTO ANNOTATION**
Draw on your comb photos with freehand pen, circles, and arrows in multiple colors before sharing with a mentor. Save annotated photos to your gallery.

**FORAGE MAPPING**
Log what's blooming near your hives with GPS pinning, a tap-to-select plant list, weather conditions, and custom notes. View all your forage entries on an interactive map.

**HIVE HEALTH CHARTS**
Track mite counts, beetle levels, queen status, and brood patterns over time with per-hive health scores that flag hives needing attention.

**OFFLINE FIRST**
Log inspections, photos, and voice notes anywhere — even without signal. Everything syncs automatically when you're back online with clear status indicators.

**DESIGNED FOR THE FIELD**
Large tap targets, voice-first logging, beginner learning mode with hints and reference images, and a dark amber theme easy on the eyes in bright sunlight.

---

Whether you're checking on your first package of bees or managing an apiary of twenty hives, Beehive Pro+ keeps your records organized, your learning on track, and your mentor in the loop.
